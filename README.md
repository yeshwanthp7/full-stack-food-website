# Smart Food Queue Management System 🍽️

## Overview
A full-stack food ordering and queue management platform built to solve crowd congestion and long waiting times during physical events, college fests, food stalls, and crowded restaurants.

This system allows users to place food orders digitally, make payments online, and track order status in real-time while admins/chefs manage food preparation and update order readiness from the admin dashboard.

---

## Problem Statement
At physical events and crowded food stalls, customers often experience:
- Long waiting queues  
- Confusion in order management  
- Delayed food collection  
- Poor crowd control  

Our platform solves this by digitizing the food ordering workflow and introducing a smart queue-based pickup system.

---

## Solution
Customers can:
- Browse available food items  
- Place orders instantly  
- Pay online via Stripe or choose Cash on Delivery  
- Track live order progress/status  

Admins/Chefs can:
- Manage food inventory  
- Add/Remove food items  
- Monitor incoming orders  
- Update order status to "Preparing", "Ready", etc.
